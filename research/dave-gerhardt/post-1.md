## Topic  
Multichannel outreach / system-based outbound  

---

## Content (Summary)

This post explains that cold outreach often fails because email and LinkedIn are treated as separate tactics instead of a connected system.

Instead of relying on a single message, prospects usually need multiple touchpoints before they respond.

---

## Key Ideas  

- Outreach should be multi-channel, not just email  
- Prospects often check LinkedIn before replying  
- Sequencing matters more than a single message  
- Timing (signals like hiring or growth) is more important than volume  
- Even good copy fails if the overall system is weak  

---

## My Observation  

This adds an important layer to cold outreach.

It’s not just about writing a good email — it’s about building a sequence across channels.  
A prospect might see your email, check your profile, and only respond after multiple interactions.

Also, timing plays a big role. Reaching out when there is a clear signal (like hiring) makes the message more relevant.

This connects well with the earlier idea that outreach is now more about systems than just messaging.
