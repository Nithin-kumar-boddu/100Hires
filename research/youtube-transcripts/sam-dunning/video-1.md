## Transcript (Excerpt)

The video explains how B2B companies can increase pipeline and revenue from SEO by focusing on high-intent, niche keywords instead of high-volume traffic.

It highlights strategies like targeting competitor-related searches, focusing on buying intent, and improving existing content to generate faster results.

## Key Ideas  

- High-intent, low-volume keywords can generate better leads than high-traffic keywords  
- Competitor-based searches (e.g., alternatives, comparisons) convert well  
- SEO should focus on business value, not just traffic  
- Existing content can be optimized to generate faster results  
- Customer research is critical to identify real pain points  

---

## Framework / Approach  

1. Identify high-intent keywords  
   - Focus on “money keywords” with buying intent  

2. Use competitor-based targeting  
   - Alternatives, comparisons, and reviews  

3. Leverage customer insights  
   - Understand real problems from sales conversations  

4. Optimize existing content  
   - Improve pages already ranking (positions 4–10)  

5. Improve conversion on pages  
   - Add clear CTAs, social proof, and examples
